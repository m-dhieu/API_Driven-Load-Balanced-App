About

Job Tracker is your personal dashboard for managing every stage of your job search.
Find new opportunities, track ongoing applications, and receive smart guidance — all in one intuitive space.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive Job Listings: Easily browse and discover jobs tailored to your profile.
  • Application Progress Tracking: Keep tabs on every application stage — submitted, interviewed, offers, and more.
  • Reminders & Notifications: Never miss an important follow-up or deadline with customizable alerts.
  • Resume & Cover Letter Management: Store and manage multiple customizable documents for each application.
  • Essay Review: Easily review your essays for grammar errors and flow for each application.
  • Data Privacy: Your information is securely stored in your browser, with zero third-party data sharing.
  • Simple & Clean Interface: Focus on your job hunt without distractions with our minimal design.

FAQ

  • Is it free? Yes, Job Tracker is free to use—no subscriptions.
  • Do I need to register? For your security and to save progress, login is required.
  • How is my data stored? Your data is saved in your browser only. No information leaves your device.
  • How does it help me? Organize applications, get reminders, improve your CV, and never miss a follow-up.
  • What if I need support? Contact us via the Support link on your dashboard after login!
